Simone — Product Design Overview

Your personal DJ. No lyrics. No playlists. Just the right vibe.

Core Identity

🎵
Simone
Not a music player. Not competing with Spotify.
An AI DJ that generates instrumental BGM for your current moment.
Lives in your menu bar. Tell her the scene, she handles the rest.

Target Users

💻
Ambient Seekers
Want background music while working, studying, cooking — without the hassle of picking songs
🎬
Content Creators (Pro)
Vloggers, podcasters, short-video creators who need royalty-free instrumental BGM

Interaction Modes

1
Set & Forget
Pick a scene, Simone plays until you stop
2
Occasional Nudge
Swipe/slide to adjust energy level on the fly
3
Auto-Evolve
Simone naturally shifts mood over time — energetic at start, mellow later

Architecture

Phase 1: MVP
macOS Menu Bar App → Direct WebSocket → Lyria RealTime API
Phase 2: App Store
macOS + iOS App → Auth Proxy (Vercel) → Lyria RealTime API
